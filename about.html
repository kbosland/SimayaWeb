About Us

The Simaya Foundation is a non profit organization that is dedicated to providing leadership opportunities to youth to build their confidence and increase their connections, as they intentionally uplift their local communities. Our annual scholarship was created to recognize the excellence of youth that belong to historically marginalized groups and support their post-secondary aspirations.

Karamjit Kaur Sangha-Bosland (she/her pronouns)/Director An educator and leader with more than 20 years of experience working in social services and public education. On an epic journey to decolonize her mind, she finds purpose and inspiration by living a life with an open heart and a sense of wonder. Devoted to human rights and equity, she amplifies the voices of youth and community engagement.
Dr Jasjit Kaur Sangha (she/her pronouns)/Director An educator who brings comprehensive experience to her work with youth through her background in adult learning, student development, mindfulness and equity and inclusion. Her knowledge of learning is also informed by her work teaching at the undergraduate and graduate level. Her training with the Search Inside Yourself Leadership Institute brings together research and teaching on emotional intelligence, neuroscience, mindfulness and leadership.
