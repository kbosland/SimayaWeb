What We Offer

Simaya Foundation Scholarship The Simaya Foundation Scholarship was established to recognize the contributions of youth in society and support their post-secondary aspirations. The scholarship consists of a one time award open to youth between the ages of 17-24.
Simaya Foundation Leadership Program The Simaya Foundation Leadership Program is designed for youth between the ages of 14-17. The purpose of this program is to provide a space in which young people are able to explore their purpose, connect with leaders from all sectors, and discover how they will contribute to their communities.
