The Annual Simaya Foundation Event

Come join us for an evening full of celebration as we raise the voices of the Simaya Youth advisory team! Simaya Foundation Scholarships will be awarded and the presentations will be followed by an interactive dance performance to encourage you onto the dance floor to move to some Bhangra, Bollywood, & Soca!


Light refreshments and appetizers will be provided!
Tickets can be bought through our EventBrite page.

Programs

Simaya Foundation Scholarship The Simaya Foundation Scholarship was created to recognize the excellence of youth that belong to a historically marginalized group in Canada and support their post-secondary aspirations. The scholarship will consist of a one time $500 award open to youth between the ages of 17-24. We are working actively with local communities when offering youth this scholarship.
Simaya Foundation Leadership Program The Simaya Foundation Leadership Program is designed for young women between the ages of 14-17. The key goal of this leadership program is to provide racialized young women with a forum in which they can access mentorship and develop their leadership capacity through self-awareness, relationship building and community engagement.
